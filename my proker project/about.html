About FindBroker

Connecting trusted brokers with customers since 2020. Building bridges for better business relationships.

Our Mission

At FindBroker, we believe in creating a transparent, secure, and efficient marketplace where customers can easily connect with verified, licensed brokers across various industries. Our mission is to eliminate the uncertainty and risk often associated with finding reliable business intermediaries.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, FindBroker emerged from the need to address the challenges customers face when searching for trustworthy brokers. Our founders, experienced professionals from the finance and technology sectors, recognized the gap in the market for a comprehensive platform that prioritizes verification, transparency, and user experience.

What started as a small team of five has grown into a dedicated organization serving thousands of customers and hundreds of verified brokers across Ethiopia and beyond.
